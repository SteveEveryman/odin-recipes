Guacamole

Description

While this is not a mexican culinary website, I am a great fan of Mexican food, even if it may at time be visually repulsive. This next dish is also a bowl a green sloppa that is delicious. You can't judge a food by it's visual quality. Even big piles of formless mush are often pretty good when made with the correct ingredients. Even though a plate of all these recipes together would look like some kind of violent bowel movement next to a dead dog, I swear they are delicious. Trust me.

Ingredients

    3 medium garlic cloves, finely chopped 1 tsp freshly ground black peppercorn 3 ripe avocados 1/2 jalapeno pepper, finely chopped 1/4 cup red onion, finely minced 1/2 cilantro leaves, chopped 2 tbsp fresh squeezed lime juice 1/2 tsp course salt

Steps

    In a medium sized bowl use a fork to smash up ripe avocados. (We like to leave ours a little big chunky.) Fold in all other ingredients and adjust salt as needed. Serve with homemade corn tortilla chips or your favorite brand of store bought chips.
