Ingredients

  • 2 lb chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup mild salsa
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 can mild diced green chiles
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tsp EACH ground cumin, garlic powder, onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp EACH smoked paprika, dry oregano, pepper
  • 1 teaspoon liquid smoke
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Rub chicken breasts with oil and place in bottom of slow cooker
  2. Add all of the ingredients into the slow cooker
  3. Cook on high for 2-4 hours or 6-7 hours until chicken is tender enough to shred
  4. Shred the chicken and let it cook on low for an additional 20 minutes to absorb juices
  5. Drain all excess liquids
  6. Enjoy
